### Energy Valuation Engine: Managed Decline Model
**Scenario-based valuation framework for integrated energy companies**  
[Repository](https://github.com/eganl2024-sudo/Energy-Valuation-Analysis)

- Developed a valuation framework designed to test whether standard terminal growth assumptions overstate long-duration hydrocarbon asset values
- Built a **managed decline** case to reflect depletion, transition risk, and lower long-run growth expectations
- Stress-tested intrinsic value across oil price, discount rate, and terminal assumption scenarios
- Used the model to examine how markets weigh **near-term cash generation versus long-term uncertainty**

**Focus:** DCF valuation, scenario analysis, terminal value sensitivity, energy transition implications

**Focus:** Market sentiment, unstructured data, financial NLP, energy market intelligence
